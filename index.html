Bio

Hi! I'm Shivam, a final-year student at BITS Pilani, Goa campus, pursuing an M.Sc. in Mathematics and a B.E. in Electronics & Communications. I am into machine learning, deep learning, and programming.

Currently, I am working as an ML Intern at Wadhwani AI, focusing on Deep Feature Synthesis and its applications. I also worked as an SDE intern at Amazon and as a research collaborator at NTU Singapore. Through my experiences, I have built skills in Machine Learning and Programming.

My Work

I am passionate about learning and constantly seeking new challenges. My interests lie in Theoretical Deep Learning, Classical Machine Learning, and Applied Machine Learning for scientific research. If you're working on something interesting, please reach out to me. I'm always eager to collaborate and contribute, whether it's on theoretical/conceptual work or practical implementation. I'm enthusiastic about learning from new experiences and projects.

Currently, I'm learning about LLMs and trying to implement a few things. I'm also planning to pick up RL once I get time.
